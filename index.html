About Finvio
Finance tools built for real life.

Finvio was born out of a simple frustration — most finance apps feel complicated and overwhelming. We wanted to build something that actually works for how people manage money day to day.

We started with the basics: a clean, simple way to track income and expenses manually. Then we added family codes so households could manage finances together. Now we're adding AI-powered insights to help everyone — from college students to small business owners — make smarter financial decisions.

Our long-term vision is to build a full financial life OS: banking integrations, investment tracking, and wealth insights — all in one app.
